Introducing DeBleve

 }

De-Bleve is a portable, remotely operated non-invasive drilling product capable of safely discharging most pressurized containers. The De-BLEVE not only provides enhanced safety, but also reduces the cost of hazardous material releases.

{ 

DeBleve Highlights

 }
  • Easily setup and operated by a single user
  • Fits most drums (including horizontal)
  • Drills through "bung lids" allowing for re-sealing
  • Can be operated from a safe distance of 100ft
  • Entirely pneumatic
  • Electro-static discharge protected
  • Lightweight aluminum frame construction
  • Can be customized to address special projects
